## The World of Sophie: A Post-Apocalyptic Journey

"SAND: Raiders of Sophie" places players in a world where the Austro-Hungarian Empire's colonial aspirations met a tragic end. The planet Sophie, once a lush, resource-rich world, is now a barren wasteland, remnants of its former grandeur scattered across the deserts. This alternate history setting, set in the year 1910, is a fascinating backdrop for a game that challenges players to adapt and survive in a harsh environment.

The game's narrative revolves around the ecological disaster that struck Sophie, forcing settlers to flee and leaving behind a world ripe for exploration and exploitation. The player is tasked with uncovering resources and treasures, navigating the treacherous terrain with a Trampler, and engaging in intense battles with other players to claim their share of the spoils.

## The Trampler: Your Mobile Fortress

The Trampler is the heart of the "SAND: Raiders of Sophie" experience. This massive, ship-like vessel serves as both a means of exploration and a storage unit for loot. Players can customize their Tramplers to suit their playstyle, from interior design to upgrading power, armor, and weapons.

The Trampler's history is as intriguing as its functionality. Originally designed in the 1870s for transporting construction materials, these large, leg-foldable vessels were instrumental in the colonization of Sophie. After the environmental disaster, they became the primary mode of transportation and, eventually, a symbol of identity and pride for those who once worked to build the planet.
