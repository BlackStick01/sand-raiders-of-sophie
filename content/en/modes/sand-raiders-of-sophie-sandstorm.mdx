# Discover the Thrilling World of SAND: Raiders of Sophie

In the desolate deserts of the fallen planet Sophie, a new era of gaming is upon us with the release of **SAND: Raiders of Sophie**. This post-apocalyptic game offers players an immersive experience where they can design, customize, and fight alongside their own Trampler in a world filled with remnants of the Austro-Hungarian Empire. Are you ready to become a **SAND raider** and explore the mysteries of Sophie's past?

## The Alluring World of Sophie

**SAND: Raiders of Sophie** places players in a world where the Austro-Hungarian Empire once colonized the planet. A mysterious ecological disaster struck, leaving Sophie in ruins. Now, players must navigate this harsh landscape, uncovering resources and treasures while facing off against other players in intense battles.

### The Tramplers: Your Mobile Fortress

The Trampler is the heart of the game, serving as both a means of exploration and a mobile base for storing loot. Players can customize their Tramplers with various upgrades, from armor and weapons to interior design. By teaming up with other players, you can create the ultimate movable base in a vast open world.

## The History and Character of Sophie

In an alternate history version of 1910, humanity had made significant advancements in space exploration, thanks to the discovery of new energy sources. The Austro-Hungarian Empire led the way in conquering other planets, but a mysterious ecological disaster struck planet Sophie, forcing its settlers to leave behind a once-resource-rich world.

### The Characters of Sophie

The game's characters originate from Galicia, a region within the Austro-Hungarian Empire predominantly inhabited by Ukrainians and Poles. During the early stages of colonization, the Kaiser promised the Galicians a significant portion of the planet Sophie, enticing them to volunteer as the primary labor force for constructing the cities that would eventually belong to them.

In addition to Galicians, our characters will also be those who come from large and small cities of the Empire - university teachers, half-crazy researchers, occultists, engineers, and former soldiers. All the different types of outcasts have their own special interest in Sophie, because her mysterious disaster beckons everyone who is eager to reveal the ambiguous secrets of cosmic Austria-Hungary.
