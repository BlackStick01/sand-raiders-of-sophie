### Surviving the Sandstorms

One of the most challenging aspects of Storm Dive is navigating the sandstorms. These fierce winds can turn a simple scavenging trip into a perilous adventure. Here are some tips to help you survive:

- **Stay Low**: The sandstorms are unpredictable, and staying low to the ground can help you avoid sudden gusts and debris.
- **Use Cover**: Find natural or constructed cover to shield yourself from the brunt of the storm.
- **Monitor Your Resources**: Keep an eye on your fuel, ammunition, and other critical resources. Running out during a sandstorm can be a death sentence.
