### Key Components of a Trampler

- **Engines and Reactors:** These power your Trampler, allowing it to traverse the treacherous dunes and withstand enemy fire.
- **Storage:** Essential for holding the loot you scavenge, storage compartments can be expanded to hold more gear.
- **Spawn Points:** These allow you to deploy other players into the world, or to spawn resources and enemies.
- **Weapons:** From turrets to heavy artillery, the right weapons can turn the tide of battle.
