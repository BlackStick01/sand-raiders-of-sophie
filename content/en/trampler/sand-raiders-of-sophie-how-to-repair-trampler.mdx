## Step-by-Step Guide to Repairing a Trampler

### 1. Assess the Damage

The first step is to assess the damage to the trampler. Check the hit markers and the condition of the trampler's systems. This will help you determine what needs to be repaired and the resources required.

### 2. Gather Repair Materials

Once you know what needs to be fixed, gather the necessary repair materials. This might include repair kits, spare parts, or specific tools required for certain repairs.

### 3. Start the Repair Process

With the materials in hand, start the repair process. This will typically involve using a repair bench or the trampler's own repair station. Be sure to follow the instructions provided to avoid causing further damage.

### 4. Test the Trampler

After the repair is complete, test the trampler to ensure it is functioning correctly. This is important to ensure that the trampler is ready for the next round of action.

## FAQ

**Q: How long does it take to repair a trampler?**

A: The repair time can vary depending on the extent of the damage and the availability of repair materials. Typically, it takes a few minutes to several hours.

**Q: Can you repair a trampler while it's still in combat?**

A: Yes, you can repair a trampler while it's still in combat, but it will be less effective. The trampler will continue to take damage while being repaired, so it's best to repair it during downtime or after the battle.

**Q: What happens if I run out of repair materials?**

A: If you run out of repair materials, you won't be able to repair the trampler until you acquire more. Be sure to keep a stock of spare parts and repair kits.
