## Optimal System Requirements for SAND: Raiders of Sophie

Welcome to the thrilling world of SAND: Raiders of Sophie, where you'll be piloting your custom-built war machines across the vast, procedurally generated desert of a fallen planet. But before you embark on your scavenging adventures, it's crucial to ensure your system can handle the intense gameplay. Here, we delve into the optimal system requirements for this action-packed, early access game.

### Minimum System Requirements

To ensure a smooth experience with the minimum system specifications, your computer should meet the following criteria:

- **Operating System:** Windows 10 64-bit
- **Processor:** Intel Core i5 (8th Gen) / AMD Ryzen 5 (2000 Series) or equivalent
- **Memory:** 16 GB RAM
- **Graphics:** NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1650, AMD Radeon RX570, Intel Arc A380
- **DirectX:** Version 11
- **Network:** Broadband Internet connection
- **Storage:** 11 GB available space

These requirements are designed to provide a baseline for gameplay, but keep in mind that you may encounter performance issues, especially during intense battles or when customizing your Trampler.

### Recommended System Requirements

For the best possible experience, consider upgrading your system to the recommended specifications:

- **Operating System:** Windows 11 64-bit
- **Processor:** Intel Core i5 (13th Gen) / AMD Ryzen 5 (7000 Series) or equivalent
- **Memory:** 32 GB RAM
- **Graphics:** NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3080, AMD Radeon RX 6800 XT
- **DirectX:** Version 11
- **Network:** Broadband Internet connection
- **Storage:** 11 GB available space

With these higher-end specifications, you can expect smoother gameplay, faster load times, and the ability to enjoy the game's stunning visuals without any hiccups.

### Performance Tips

Even with the recommended system requirements, there are several tips you can follow to enhance your performance in SAND: Raiders of Sophie:

- **Update Your Drivers:** Keep your graphics and network drivers up to date for optimal performance.
- **Close Background Applications:** Running unnecessary applications in the background can consume system resources and slow down your gameplay.
- **Adjust Graphics Settings:** Lowering the graphics settings can help improve performance, especially on lower-end systems.
- **Use a SSD:** If possible, use a solid-state drive (SSD) instead of a traditional hard disk drive (HDD) for faster load times.
