Welcome to the thrilling world of SAND: Raiders of Sophie, where the dunes of an alternate 1910 are the stage for a battle royale like no other. This procedurally generated desert offers a vast landscape to explore, a treasure trove of loot to scavenge, and a fierce community of players ready to clash in a brutal PvPvE experience. ### Crafting Your Fortress: The Trampler Editor

At the heart of SAND: Raiders of Sophie is the Trampler Editor, a deep and comprehensive system that allows you to construct your own walking mech fortress. This isn't just any mech; it's your shield, your defense, and your weapon. Here's how to get started:

- **Choose Your Base:** Start with the basic compartments like engines, reactors, storage, and spawn points.
- **Add Weapons and Defenses:** Equip your trampler with turrets, cannons, and other weapons to protect yourself and your loot.
- **Customize Your Design:** Whether you want a stealthy, agile design or a massive, impenetrable fortress, the editor gives you the tools to make it happen.
- **Save Your Blueprint:** In the heat of battle, your trampler can be destroyed. Save your design as a blueprint to rebuild quickly and get back to the action.

### Exploring the World of Sophie

SAND: Raiders of Sophie boasts an expansive world with procedural layouts and multiple cities to explore. Here are some tips to help you navigate this vast desert:

- **Scavenge for Resources:** Gather loot, weapons, and artifacts by exploring cities, ruins, and shipwrecks.
- **Complete Contracts:** Take on contracts for valuable rewards and experience. Contracts can range from simple scavenging missions to more complex objectives.
- **Be Aware of Upiors:** The supernatural Upiors roam the dunes. Engage with them wisely, as they can be both allies and enemies.
- **PvP and PvE:** Engage in battles with other players or face off against AI-controlled enemies. The choice is yours.

### Playing Solo or in a Squad

SAND: Raiders of Sophie offers both solo and multiplayer experiences. Here's how to make the most of both:

- **Go Solo:** If you prefer to play alone, focus on building your trampler and exploring the world at your own pace.
- **Team Up:** Playing with friends can make the experience more fun and strategic. Work together to take down enemies and secure valuable loot.

### Surviving the Storm Dive

The Storm Dive mode in SAND: Raiders of Sophie raises the stakes. Here's what you need to know:

- **Higher-Tier Loot:** Storm Dive offers access to higher-tier loot and greater rewards, but it's also riskier.
- **Sandstorms:** As sandstorms engulf the region, the risk of confrontations with other scavengers and the loss of your loot increases.
- **Prepare for the Worst:** Make sure your trampler is fully equipped and your skills are sharp before diving into Storm Dive.
