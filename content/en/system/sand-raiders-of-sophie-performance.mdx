### Understanding the World of Sophie

SAND: Raiders of Sophie takes place on the post-apocalyptic planet Sophie, where players embark on a quest to uncover resources and treasures. The world is vast, filled with remnants of the Austro-Hungarian Empire's luxurious past. To navigate this challenging terrain, players are equipped with Tramplers, walking mechs capable of traversing the islands once submerged beneath a vast sea.

The Tramplers serve as the player's main base, providing a means of exploration and loot storage. With the ability to customize their Tramplers from interior design to upgrading power, armor, and weapons, players can create a unique and powerful base of operations.

### Customization: The Heart of Performance

One of the most critical aspects of mastering the performance in SAND: Raiders of Sophie is understanding how to customize your Tramplers. By investing in the right upgrades, you can enhance your mobility, protection, and combat capabilities.

**Here are some key upgrades to consider:**

| Upgrade Type | Description | Impact on Performance |

### Teamwork: The Power of Collaboration

SAND: Raiders of Sophie is not just about individual performance; it's also about teamwork. By joining forces with other players, you can create the ultimate movable base and pool resources to achieve greater success.

- **Resource Management**: Share resources wisely to ensure that everyone has what they need to thrive.
