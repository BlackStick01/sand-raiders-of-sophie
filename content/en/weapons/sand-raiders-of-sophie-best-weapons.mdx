### Understanding the Gameplay

"SAND" has been an extraction shooter since its inception, but it offers a twist that sets it apart from the usual fare. The game boasts two distinct modes: **Voyage mode** and **Storm dive**. In Voyage mode, players can enjoy a persistent server experience with no time limit, allowing for a more casual, exploration-centric gameplay. Conversely, Storm dive mode offers session-based gameplay with shorter rounds and higher-tier weapons, all while dealing with the ever-shrinking map due to a relentless sandstorm.
