Welcome to the unforgiving dunes of Sophie, where the survival of the fittest reigns supreme in the critically acclaimed game, SAND: Raiders of Sophie. Developed by Hologryph and published by tinyBuild, this action-adventure title puts players in the boots of rugged scavengers, piloting their own customizable walking mechs, or Trampers, in a procedurally generated desert setting. Among the chaos and loot lies an arsenal of weapons designed to turn the tide of battle. Let's delve into the arsenal of SAND: Raiders of Sophie and learn how to wield them effectively.

### The Core of Your Power: Trampers

Before we dive into the weapons, it's crucial to understand the heart of your combat strategy: your Trampler. Each Trampler is a highly customizable walking fortress, built from various compartments such as engines, reactors, storage, spawn points, and, of course, weapons. The Trampler you choose and the weapons you outfit it with will shape your playstyle and success on the battlefield.

#### Crafting Your Arsenal

To build your arsenal, you'll need to use the Trampler Editor, a comprehensive system that allows you to construct your Trampler from the ground up. Here's a brief overview of the process:

- **Start with a chassis**: Choose a base chassis that suits your playstyle, whether it's a sturdy combat frame or a sleek scout model.
- **Add compartments**: Customize your Trampler by adding compartments like turrets, reactors, and storage units. The variety is endless, so choose wisely.
- **Arm your weapons**: Select from a variety of weapons that fit your playstyle. From rapid-firing machine guns to heavy-duty autocannons, there's a weapon for every situation.
- **Save your blueprint**: Once you've constructed your perfect Trampler, save your blueprint so you can quickly rebuild it after it's been destroyed in battle.
