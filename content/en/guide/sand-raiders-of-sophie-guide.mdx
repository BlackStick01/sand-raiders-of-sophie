## Crafting Your Trampler: The Heart of Your Raider

The **Trampler** is the centerpiece of your raider experience in **SAND: Raiders of Sophie**. This highly customizable walking mech fortress is your primary tool for survival, defense, and offense. Here's how to craft the perfect Trampler:

- **Start with the Basics**: Begin by choosing the core compartments for your Trampler, such as engines, reactors, and storage.
- **Add Weapons and Turrets**: Beef up your defenses with a variety of weapons and turrets. From heavy machine guns to rocket launchers, the choice is yours.
- **Optimize for Performance**: Ensure your Trampler is well-balanced. Too much firepower can lead to overheating, while too much armor can slow you down.

### Player Experience

According to player reports, many have found that starting with a simple, yet sturdy Trampler allows for better maneuverability and adaptability as the game progresses.
