### Understanding the Gameplay

SAND: Raiders of Sophie is a game that combines the thrill of exploration, building, and combat in a massive procedurally generated desert. Here's a quick rundown of the main gameplay elements:

- **Customizable Tramplers:** Build your own walking mech fortress, known as a Trampler, from the ground up. Customize it with various compartments, weapons, and reactors.
- **Exploration:** Roam the expansive world, uncovering secrets, completing contracts, and engaging in both Player vs. Player (PvP) and Player vs. Environment (PvE) battles.
- **Looting and Resources:** Scavenge for weapons, loot, and artifacts to strengthen your Trampler and ensure your survival.
- **Survival:** Navigating the treacherous desert isn't easy. Keep an eye out for Upiors, other players, and environmental hazards.
- **Extraction:** Once you've gathered enough resources and loot, it's time to extract to safety at the orbital station.

### How to Play with Friends

Playing with friends can significantly enhance your experience in SAND: Raiders of Sophie. Here's how to set up and enjoy your group adventure:

#### 1. Choose Your Tramplers

First, design your Tramplers. You can build them from scratch using the comprehensive Trampler editor system. Consider the roles each Trampler will play—some might be focused on defense, while others prioritize speed and agility.

#### 2. Coordinate Your Strategy

Discuss your strategy with your friends before deploying into the world. Decide whether you'll focus on resource gathering, combat, or exploration. Having a clear plan will make your group more efficient and effective.

#### 3. Communicate

Communication is key in SAND: Raiders of Sophie. Use in-game chat to coordinate movements, report threats, and share information about loot locations.

#### 4. Join a Match

To play with friends, you need to join a match together. You can start a private match for your group or join a public match with friends.

#### 5. Work Together

Collaboration is crucial for survival. Work together to defend against enemies and Upiors, and share resources and loot.
