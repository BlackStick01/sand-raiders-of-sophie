## Core Mechanics Explained: SAND Raiders of Sophie

Understanding the inner workings of your **Trampler** is crucial before customizing its loadout. Since the game is in Early Access, expect rapid shifts in balance and stat values. Here's what you need to master:

- **The Reactor Core Loop:** Your **Trampler** is powered by the Reactor, which is its heart. Targeting an enemy's Reactor is the fastest way to disable or destroy their vessel. Keep your Reactor well-protected.
- **Modular Customization:** Every **Trampler** is built from individual modules. You can alter the chassis size, add storage, and mount external gun platforms. Heavy modules increase defense but reduce speed.
- **Extraction Mechanics:** All resources, weapons, and black boxes must be stored properly. If you die without reaching an extraction tower, only properly stored items return to orbit.
- **The Sandbox Threat (PvPvE):** Besides fighting players, you'll encounter Upiors, supernatural creatures. Your defenses must be robust enough to handle them.

## How to Get a New Trampler in SAND Raiders of Sophie

Losing a **Trampler** can be devastating, but getting a replacement is straightforward. Here's how:

1. Return to the Orbital Hangar: Make sure you've extracted safely and open your main terminal interface.
2. Access the Blueprint Registry: Navigate to the Trampler Editor menu to select a custom blueprint or a standard template.
3. Purchase Modules with Scavenged Resources: Spend your gathered currency, metal plates, and raw components to purchase the necessary structural modules.
4. Assemble the Chassis Blocks: Use the editor to lock your modular compartments together, ensuring secure paths to internal crew beds and steering controls.
5. Mount External Turrets and Deploy: Place your chosen Autocannons or Flak guns onto the external mounting sockets. Load up your inventory and select the mode for deployment.

## FAQs About SAND Raiders of Sophie

### Can you steal ships in SAND Raiders of Sophie?
Yes, you can board and take control of rival **Tramplers** during a match. However, you cannot permanently save a stolen ship into your collection.

### What happens if my Trampler gets completely destroyed?
If your **Trampler** is destroyed, you lose the physical instance and any unshielded loot. However, you retain your unlocked blueprints in the orbital hangar.

### Where is the best place to find high-tier weapons and attachments?
The most valuable weapons are typically hidden behind locked red security doors inside the industrial ruins of major desert cities.

### How does weight affect my overall Trampler movement?
Every structural module and heavy iron armor plate adds mass to your **Trampler**, potentially reducing speed. Balance your defensive layout with upgraded engine modules.

### What is the difference between Voyage Mode and Storm Dive?
Voyage Mode is lower-risk for testing blueprints, while Storm Dive is high-stakes with better loot drops but more aggressive PvP conflicts.

### What are Upiors and how do I deal with them?
Upiors are aggressive creatures that roam the streets of Sophie. The most effective way to handle them is to lure them into open terrain and eliminate them with your ship's Flak Artillery.
