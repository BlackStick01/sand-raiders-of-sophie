## Crafting Your Trampler: The Foundation of Your Success

The heart of **SAND: Raiders of Sophie** is your custom-built trampler. This towering mech is your fortress, your weapon, and your mode of transportation. When building your trampler, focus on a design that suits your playstyle:

- **Balanced Power**: Invest in engines, reactors, and weapons to ensure your trampler is both agile and formidable.
- **Storage Solutions**: Maximize your storage capacity to hold essential loot, weapons, and supplies.
- **Stealthy Design**: If you prefer avoiding direct conflict, a stealthy trampler with concealed weapons can be your best bet.

Player experience has shown that a well-crafted trampler can mean the difference between survival and extraction, or becoming a meal for the supernatural Upiors.

## Scavenging and Resource Management

Scavenge the dunes for valuable resources to upgrade your trampler and stay ahead of the curve. Here are some key points to consider:

- **Resource Priority**: Prioritize collecting essential resources such as fuel, ammunition, and medical supplies.
- **Loot Locations**: Keep an eye out for resource-rich areas and abandoned facilities for a bounty of loot.
- **Inventory Management**: Regularly check and manage your inventory to ensure you have what you need without overloading your trampler.

## Surviving the Desert: Navigating the World

The world of **SAND: Raiders of Sophie** is vast and filled with challenges. Here are some survival tips:

- **Weather Awareness**: The game features a full day/night cycle and unpredictable weather patterns. Plan your travels accordingly.
- **PvP Strategy**: Engage in PvP with caution. Assess the situation before initiating combat, and be prepared for a quick retreat.
- **PvE Encounters**: Tread carefully when encountering Upiors or other scavengers. Use stealth and your trampler's defenses to your advantage.

## Building Your Base: Creating a Haven

Constructing a base can be a significant advantage in **SAND: Raiders of Sophie**. Follow these steps to create a safe haven:

- **Strategic Location**: Choose a spot that is defensible and close to essential resources.
- **Fortification**: Use available materials to fortify your base with turrets and defenses.
- **Upgrades and Amenities**: Build workshops and other amenities to improve your base and support your long-term survival.
