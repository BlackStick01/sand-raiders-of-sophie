### Crafting Your Perfect Trampler

The heart of your success in **SAND** is your trampler, the colossal walking mech that serves as your base of operations. A well-designed trampler can be the difference between a fruitful scavenging trip and a devastating defeat. Here are some tips to build the perfect trampler:

- **Modular Design**: Use the trampler editor to construct a modular design. Think about your playstyle and the challenges you'll face. Do you need heavy armor and a powerful weapon for defense? Or perhaps you want to be agile and stealthy to avoid detection.
- **Resource Management**: Balance your trampler's weight and power. Too much weight will slow you down, and too much power will drain your resources quickly. Keep an eye on your energy levels and manage your cargo efficiently.
- **Customization**: Personalize your trampler with different compartments, weapons, and aesthetics. Your trampler is a reflection of your identity in this unforgiving world.

### Scavenging the Desert

Once you've crafted your trampler, it's time to hit the desert and start scavenging. Here are some strategies to help you gather the most valuable loot:

- **Explore Procedurally Generated Areas**: The game features an expansive world with procedural layouts, meaning every playthrough is unique. Explore different areas to find new loot and resources.
- **Complete Contracts**: Contracts offer a structured way to gather loot. They provide clear objectives and can lead you to hidden caches and valuable artifacts.
- **PvP and PvE Encounters**: Engaging with other players and the supernatural Upiors can lead to both rewards and risks. Be strategic in your encounters to maximize your loot.
